Diane Tchuindjo

 

I am a first year graduate student in the Data Science Department at the University of California, San Diego. My research interests lie broadly in probability theory, statistics, and optimization.

Previously, I completed my undergraduate degree in mathematics and economics at the University of Maryland, College Park. During my time as an undergraduate student I interned at the Mathematical Sciences Research Institute, the Department of Defense, and Microsoft Quantum. After graduating, I spent some time as a research engineer and mathematician at Apogee Research and Envieta, respectively.

Incoming News: I am currently spending part of my time at Redpoll AI working on probabilistic ML!
